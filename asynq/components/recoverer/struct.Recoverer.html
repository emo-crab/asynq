Recoverer - 负责恢复孤儿任务 Recoverer - responsible for recovering orphaned tasks

对应 Go asynq 的 recoverer 组件 Corresponds to Go asynq’s recoverer component

孤儿任务是指在 “active” 状态中，但其对应的工作者已经崩溃或超时的任务。 Orphaned tasks are tasks in the “active” state whose corresponding worker has crashed or timed out. Recoverer 会定期扫描这些任务并将它们重新加入队列。 The Recoverer periodically scans for these tasks and re-queues them.
